Apple Bavarian Torte (★★★★)

A classic European torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apple make this the perfect holiday tart (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 apples - peeled, cored and sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Cream together butter, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla; add flour and mix well. Press into the bottom and 1 1/2 inches up the side of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Mix together the cream cheese, 1/4 cup sugar, egg, and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla. Pour into the crust.
  4. Toss apples with cinnamon and 1/4 cup sugar; spoon over the cream cheese layer.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400°F (200°C) and continue baking for 25 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle almonds on top of the torte, and continue baking for another 10 minutes.
  7. Allow the torte to cool in the pan on a wire rack. Serve chilled.
